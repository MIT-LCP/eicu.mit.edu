# eICU Collaborative Research Database

The [eICU Collaborative Research Database](https://eicu-crd.mit.edu) is a large multi-center critical care database made available by [Philips Healthcare](http://www.usa.philips.com/healthcare/product/HCNOCTN503/eicu-program-telehealth-for-the-intensive-care-unit) in partnership with the [MIT Laboratory for Computational Physiology](https://lcp.mit.edu/).

The database holds data associated with over 200,000 patient stays, providing a large sample size for research studies.
